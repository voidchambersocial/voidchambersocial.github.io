Cracked

Feel the adrenaline rush in Cracked

A formidable 2D platformer adventure that will test your skills to the limit as you traverse the eerie landscapes of an abandoned Factory.

Embark on a Journey Through Diverse Locations

Choose your own path through a rich tapestry of locations, each with its own unique challenges. It's your call which one to tackle first, adding a layer of strategy to your adventure.

Craft Your Unique Character

Take control of your destiny by customizing your character to match your style and preferences. Enhance your abilities and appearance to make a truly personalized gaming experience.

Rise to the Top of the Leaderboards

Compete for glory by racing against time to complete the maps with lightning speed. Aim for the highest spot on the leaderboards, where you can challenge your own records, compete with friends, or prove your skills against players from across the globe.

Defeat the Unyielding Boss

In a heroic effort, vanquish the formidable boss waiting at the end of each campaign. Your success not only saves your friends but also unlocks them as playable characters, allowing you to make the crucial choice of who to rescue first.

In Cracked, every move counts as you navigate treacherous terrain, customize your character, and strive for leaderboard dominance. Will you have what it takes to conquer this challenging 2D platformer and emerge victorious?
